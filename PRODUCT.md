# r6Hub — Product Context

## Users
Competitive and casual Rainbow Six Siege players who need tactical coordination during matches. Team leaders who organize strategies. Players joining lobbies for coordinated gameplay.

## Product Purpose
Second-screen tactical companion app for Rainbow Six Siege. Enables real-time team coordination: operator bans, map selection, strategy submission, and task assignment during matches.

## Brand Tone
- **Modern minimal** — Linear.app inspired, clean, professional
- **2026 aesthetic** — Forward-looking, not retro gaming
- **Client-facing** — Polished, trustworthy, enterprise-grade feel
- **Guided UX** — Clear flow, no confusion, intuitive navigation
- **Confident** — Bold but restrained, not playful or childish

## Anti-References
- Generic gaming aesthetics (neon, RGB, aggressive angles)
- Cluttered dashboards with too much information
- Playful/cartoonish gaming apps
- Outdated Web 2.0 design patterns

## Strategic Principles
1. **Clarity over density** — Show only what's needed, when it's needed
2. **Speed to action** — Minimize clicks from landing to gameplay
3. **Visual hierarchy** — Clear primary/secondary/tertiary distinction
4. **Consistent patterns** — Same interaction patterns across all pages
5. **Responsive first** — Mobile-friendly for second-screen use during matches

## Register
**Product** — This is an app UI, not a marketing site. Design serves functionality.
